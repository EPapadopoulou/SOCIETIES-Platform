Privacy Assessment

  • To assess possible transmission of your data by installed services, the platform monitors any data access or data transmission by services, and calculates privacy assessment metrics.

    To view these metrics for each identity or software component, select the result type from the drop-down list below and click "Send" at the bottom of page.

    Note that a high value does not necessarily indicate a malicious service, or a service that violates the service level agreement or the privacy policy. Keep in mind the correlation results are only an assessment, and many services may be accessing and/or transmitting your data in scope of their normal operation. It is up to you to decide if data access and data transmission are excessive or not.
